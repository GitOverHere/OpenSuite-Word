Pong

Pong is a game played with two paddles and one ball. These paddles will hit the ball back and forward until one of these paddles fails to make the ball bounce back. When you fail to hit the ball, the AI gets one point. Whoever gets 10 points wins. You can adjust the difficulty of the game easily by using the AI slider in the settings menu.
